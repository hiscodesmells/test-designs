About Us

We help improve the quality of health care delivered to Native American communities through expert revenue cycle management.

Native Command Consulting is a 100% Native American-owned-and-operated professional consulting services company headquartered in Pierre, South Dakota. Since 2013, we have dedicated ourselves to partnering with Indian Health Service facilities and tribal health care organizations.

Our primary focus is Revenue Cycle Management — ensuring that tribal health facilities receive the reimbursements they are entitled to, allowing them to reinvest in delivering exceptional patient care to their communities. Every dollar recovered is a dollar returned to health care.

“Every dollar we help recover goes directly back into improving health care for Native American communities. That is not just our work — it is our mission.”

What We Do

Our Services

01 Health Information Management

We help tribal health care facilities manage, maintain, and optimize their health information systems. From transitioning to paperless environments to ensuring full regulatory compliance, our team brings deep expertise in health records management tailored to IHS and tribal facility needs.

  • Health records management and organization
  • Paperless transition consulting
  • Compliance and regulatory adherence
  • Data integrity and quality assurance
  • Staff training and workflow optimization
02 Medical Coding & Billing

Our certified medical coders ensure accurate and timely processing of payment information. Every coder on our team is AAPC or AHIMA certified with a minimum of 3 years of hands-on experience, specializing in the unique coding requirements of Indian Health Service facilities.

  • AAPC and AHIMA certified professionals
  • Minimum 3+ years coding experience required
  • Accurate payment information processing
  • Revenue optimization and recovery
  • Coding audits and compliance reviews
03 Electronic Content Management

Through our strategic partnership with Laserfiche, we deliver enterprise-level document and process automation solutions. We help facilities digitize, organize, and automate their document workflows, dramatically improving efficiency and reducing manual processes.

  • Official Laserfiche partnership
  • Document automation and digitization
  • Process automation workflows
  • Enterprise content management
  • Integration with existing health systems
04 Business Process Engineering

We analyze and redesign operational workflows to help tribal health facilities streamline their operations, reduce costs, and increase productivity. Our engineers identify bottlenecks and implement solutions that deliver measurable improvements in facility performance.

  • Operational workflow analysis
  • Cost reduction strategies
  • Productivity improvement planning
  • Process optimization and redesign
  • Performance metrics and reporting
