About Us

Rooted in Community, Driven by Purpose

Native Command Consulting was founded in 2013 with a singular mission: to help improve the quality of health care available to Native American communities across the nation.

As a 100% Native American-owned-and-operated consulting firm based in Pierre, South Dakota, we understand the unique challenges facing Indian Health Service and tribal health care facilities. Our team specializes in Revenue Cycle Management, bringing deep expertise and cultural understanding to every engagement.

We bridge the gap between operational efficiency and community care, ensuring that health facilities have the financial foundation they need to serve their communities with excellence.

Trusted Partner Since 2013

Over a decade of dedicated service to tribal health facilities, building lasting relationships and delivering measurable results in revenue cycle performance.
