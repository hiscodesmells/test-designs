About Us

A Consulting Firm Built on Purpose, Not Just Profit

“Our mission is to help improve the quality of health care for Native American communities through expert consulting and innovative technology.”
2013 Year
Established

Native Command Consulting is a 100% Native American-owned-and-operated professional consulting firm based in Pierre, South Dakota. Since our founding in 2013, we have dedicated ourselves to one clear purpose: strengthening the health care infrastructure that serves Native American communities across the nation.

Our primary focus is Revenue Cycle Management for Indian Health Service (IHS) and tribal health care facilities. We understand the unique regulatory environment, the specific challenges of tribal health systems, and the critical importance of every dollar recovered in funding quality patient care.

We bring together certified coding professionals, health information management experts, and technology specialists who share a deep commitment to the communities we serve. Our team does not simply consult — we embed ourselves as partners in your mission to deliver better health outcomes.
