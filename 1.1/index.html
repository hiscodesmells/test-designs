Our Story

A Mission Rooted in
Community & Care

Native Command Consulting (NCC) is a 100% Native American-owned-and-operated professional consulting firm headquartered in Pierre, South Dakota. Founded in 2013, we bring over a decade of specialized expertise in Revenue Cycle Management tailored exclusively to IHS and tribal health care facilities.

"Our mission is to improve the quality of health care available to Native American communities through expert consulting, innovative technology, and unwavering dedication."

We understand the unique challenges facing Indian Health Service and tribal health care systems. Our team of specialized coders and consultants delivers solutions that directly improve revenue cycles, operational efficiency, and ultimately, patient care.
