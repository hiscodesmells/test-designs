02 / About

Improving the quality of health care for Native American communities

We believe that every tribal community deserves world-class health care administration — and we’re building the systems to make it happen.

Native Command Consulting

Since 2013, NCC has been dedicated to improving health care outcomes through superior Revenue Cycle Management. As a 100% Native American-owned-and-operated firm based in Pierre, South Dakota, we bring a uniquely informed perspective to every engagement with IHS and tribal health care facilities.

Our team of specialized professionals understands the distinct regulatory frameworks, cultural considerations, and operational challenges faced by Indian Health Service and tribal health care organizations. This deep institutional knowledge allows us to deliver consulting solutions that are not only technically excellent but also culturally relevant.

We partner with facilities across the nation — from Alaska to Arizona, New Mexico to South Dakota — providing comprehensive consulting services that span the full spectrum of revenue cycle operations. Our approach combines proven methodologies with innovative technology to drive measurable improvements in financial performance and operational efficiency.

Every engagement begins with a thorough assessment of current operations and concludes with sustainable systems that continue to deliver results long after our direct involvement ends. This commitment to lasting impact is what sets NCC apart.
